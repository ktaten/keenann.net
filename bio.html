FIRST & FOREMOST... THANK YOU FOR BEING HERE.

My name is Keenan Tate Normington (b. 2002). I am a fine art photographer based in Wisconsin.

I am a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee (Peck School of the Arts) pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Photography & Imaging. I will be graduating in Fall 2025.

In the realm of photography, my interests span across various genres including portraiture, landscape, street photography, and the use of alternative materials/processes. I utilize both film and digital processes, taking special interest in the use of black and white imagery.

Within my work, I explore the landscapes and people that make up my world. My goal is to capture the beauty of our world, and the connections that bind us to one another.

My current focus is on a long term project and in progress photo book titled Midwest. Within this work, I explore the landscapes of the American Midwest. Inspired by the people, culture, and rural areas of the Midwest, my goal is to capture the essence of a region I’ve called home throughout my entire life. My lifelong connection to the Midwest imbues this project with a sense of intimacy and authenticity. Through this work I hope to share not only images of the Midwest’s beauty and character, but a profound connection to a place I proudly call home.

IT IS A PLEASURE SHARING MY WORK WITH YOU.
