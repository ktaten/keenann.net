“MIDWEST”, DIGITAL & FILM, 2022-PRESENT.

Photo book is in progress, and will be available Spring 2026.

In "Midwest," my goal is to capture the multifaceted essence of a region I've called home throughout my entire life. Inspired by the landscapes, people, and culture that define the Midwest, this project is a visual exploration of its beauty and character.

As someone deeply rooted in the Midwest, my lifelong connection to it imbues this project with a sense of intimacy and authenticity. Through my lens, I hope to share not just images, but a profound connection to a place I proudly call home.
