Wisconsin, 35mm/120/4x5 film & digital, 2022-present.

Wisconsin Wisconsin is an ongoing project that explores the landscapes and people of my home state.

Focusing on the rural areas of Wisconsin, my goal is to shed new light on their beauty and character. While also illuminating the connection between people and their environment.
