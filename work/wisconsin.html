[IN PROGRESS]

Wisconsin, 35mm/120/4x5 film & digital, 2022-present.

Wisconsin is an ongoing project and photo book that unearths the landscapes, people, and culture of my home state.

Through this body of work, I seek to illuminate the beauty and distinct character of Wisconsin by exploring my personal connection to its landscapes and communities.

In capturing the essence of Wisconsin and the lived experiences of myself and its people, I aim to evoke a sense of place, memory, and belonging. Through these images, I offer viewers an intimate reflection on Wisconsin’s identity and the profound relationship between people and their environment.
