[IN PROGRESS]

Midwest, film & digital, 2022-present.

In Midwest, my goal is to capture the multifaceted essence of a region I've called home throughout my entire life. Inspired by the landscapes, people, and culture that define the American Midwest, this book is a visual exploration of its beauty and character.

As someone deeply rooted in the Midwest, my lifelong connection to it imbues this project with a sense of intimacy and authenticity. Through this work, I hope to share not just images, but a profound connection to a place I proudly call home.
